# KidQuest — Project Brief

## What It Is

KidQuest is a long-term educational game that grows together with Chopin (age 5).

Children experience play first. Learning happens naturally through missions, exploration, and a living creature they care for.

## Primary User

**Chopin** — born July 18, 2020. Loves Sonic, Minecraft, Pokémon. Learns through play, not passive instruction.

## The Golden Rule

> Build one mastery level ahead. Never six years ahead.

The project stays approximately one mastery level ahead of where the child currently is.  
Year 1 focus: **Kindergarten core + Early Grade 1 stretch.**

## Success Metric

Chopin voluntarily plays KidQuest and learns through it.

Not feature count. Not content volume. Not grades covered. **Joyful learning.**

## What It Is Not

KidQuest is not trying to become Khan Academy Kids, Duolingo, ABCmouse, or a complete K-6 platform. It grows gradually over many years alongside one child.
