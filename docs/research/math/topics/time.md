# Time

## Purpose

Help children understand the passage of time, read clocks, and organize daily events, building the foundation for calendars, schedules, elapsed time, and problem solving.

---

## Core Concepts

Time helps us describe:

- when things happen
- how long things last
- the order of events

Examples:

- morning
- afternoon
- evening
- yesterday
- today
- tomorrow

---

## Common Misconceptions

### Bigger Hand Means Hour Hand

Incorrect.

The shorter hand shows hours.

---

### Time Always Moves by Whole Hours

Children gradually learn:

- half hours
- quarter hours
- minutes

---

### Reading Digital Clocks Incorrectly

Example:

08:05

Not:

8:50

---

### Confusing AM and PM

Morning and evening times differ.

## Teacher Notes

Begin with daily routines before introducing clocks.

Use concrete experiences.

Connect time to meaningful events.

---

## Parent Notes

Discuss time naturally.

Examples:

- bedtime
- meals
- school schedules
- holidays

Ask:

"What happens first?"

"How long will it take?"

"What time is it now?"
