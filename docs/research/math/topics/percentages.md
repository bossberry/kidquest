# Percentages

## Purpose

Help children understand percentages as parts out of 100.

---

## Core Concepts

100% means the whole.

50% means half.

25% means one quarter.

75% means three quarters.

---

## Everyday Examples

### Battery

100%

50%

20%

---

### Discounts

20% off

50% off

---

### School Scores

80%

95%

100%

---

## Visual Models

Use:

- grids
- bars
- circles

with 100 sections.

---

## Learning Progression

### Understand 100%

Whole object

---

### Understand 50%

Half

---

### Understand 25%

Quarter

---

### Understand 10%

Ten out of one hundred

---

## Connections

50% = 1/2

25% = 1/4

0.5 = 50%

0.25 = 25%

---

## Common Misconceptions

Children may think:

80% means 80 objects.

Correct:

80 out of every 100.
